Frequently Asked Questions

Is a Fayda ID required?

Yes. To access our 2026 job placement services, you must provide a Fayda ID for student verification.

What skills will I learn?

We focus on high-demand skills: Python Programming, AI Prompt Engineering, and Remote Collaboration tools.

Are there any costs?

Our foundational courses are free. Advanced certification tracks are subsidized for high-performing students.

How long are the programs?

Standard tracks run for 12 weeks, with flexible evening sessions designed for active students.

Can I work for international companies?

Yes, our primary goal is to connect trained Ethiopian developers with global remote work opportunities in 2026.
