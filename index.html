The Problem

Ethiopian talent is vast, but students often face a critical disconnect from the 2026 global economy. Traditional training systems frequently lack access to high-demand AI and digital skills. Furthermore, without a verified professional identity, many students struggle to enter the competitive international remote job market, leaving local potential untapped.

Our Solution

ልማት Hub acts as a transformative gateway. We offer customized, AI-powered learning paths that focus specifically on the skills global companies need right now. By integrating with Ethiopia's National ID (Fayda), we provide a secure way for students to verify their credentials and identity, making it easier than ever to match local expertise with global opportunities.
